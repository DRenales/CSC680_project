The intention of this project was to revive an old project some coleagues of mine had begun during our undergrad at UCR.
This webpage should serve as a POC for the visualization of data originally processed within an Apache Spark db console.
The graphical data page will provide filter-able columns for observing continuous data acquired from the WHO
The barred data page will provide filter-able columns for comparing simple statistics against other countries
